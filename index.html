About me

how i became fullstack developer?

My journey to becoming a fullstack web developer was shaped by a rich blend of self-learning and structured education. I kickstarted my coding adventure by delving into tutorials from Web Programming UNPAS on YouTube, absorbing the fundamentals of HTML, CSS, and JavaScript.

Seeking a more structured approach, I enrolled in Harisenin.com's intensive bootcamp. Through hands-on projects and immersive learning experiences, I gained proficiency in both front-end and back-end technologies. The bootcamp provided a structured curriculum, mentorship, and a supportive community, accelerating my growth.

Additional learning resources, including FreeCodeCamp and W3Schools, complemented my journey. Regularly engaging with diverse coding challenges and projects deepened my understanding and honed my problem-solving skills. Each learning avenue contributed to my evolution into a versatile and confident fullstack web developer.
